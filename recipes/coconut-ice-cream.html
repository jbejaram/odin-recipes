Coconut Ice Cream Recipe

Source: Wikimedia Commons

Description

A creamy, tropical coconut ice cream made with rich coconut milk and a hint of sweetness — smooth, refreshing, and perfect for warm days.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut cream o thick coconut milk
  • 1 cup regular milk (or water if you want them softer)
  • 4–5 tablespoons sugar, honey, o cane sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Blend everything until smooth.
  2. Taste — if it’s not sweet enough, add a little more sugar.
  3. Pour into popsicle molds.
  4. Freeze at least 6 hours or overnight.
