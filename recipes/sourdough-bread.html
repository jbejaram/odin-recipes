Sourdough Bread Recipe

Source: Wikimedia Commons

Description

This sourdough bread recipe uses a naturally fermented starter to create a rustic loaf with a crisp crust and soft, tangy interior. Made with just flour, water, and salt, it highlights the natural flavors of slow fermentation, resulting in a wholesome, artisan-style bread thatâ€™s perfect for everyday baking.

Ingredients:

  • Flour: 900g
  • Water: 630ml
  • Salt: 18g
  • Sourdough: 180g

Instructions:

  1. Mix the water and the flour.
  2. Let the dough rest during 1 hour and cover it with a kitchen towel.
  3. Add the Sourdough and the salt to the dough.
  4. Stretch and fold the ingredients are fully incorporated (the mixture should be homogeneous).
  5. Let the dough rest during 30 minutes and cover it with a kitchen towel.
  6. Stretch and fold the dough again (4 or 5 times)
  7. Let the dough rest during 30 minutes and cover it with a kitchen towel.
  8. Stretch and fold the dough again (4 or 5 times)
  9. Let the dough rest during 30 minutes and cover it with a kitchen towel.
  10. Stretch and fold the dough again (4 or 5 times)
  11. Let the dough rest during 30 minutes and cover it with a kitchen towel.
  12. Stretch and fold the dough again (4 or 5 times)
  13. Let the dough rest during 3 hours and cover it with a kitchen towel.
  14. Pour flour over a surface where you will mass the dough to shape it as a ball.
  15. Let it rest another hour and cover it with a kitchen towel.
  16. Place the dough in a floured bowl and cover the bowl with plastic.
  17. Refrigerate overnight (between 12~16 hours).
  18. Preheat the oven at 250 Celsius degrees with a Dutch Oven inside
  19. Take out the dough from the fridge and slice the top of it, the put it into the Dutch Oven preheated and bake during 30 minutes at 250 Celsius degrees.
  20. Bake another 25 minutes at the same temperature, but without the Dutch Oven lid.
  21. Take the bread out and let it cool over a grill at least 1 hour.
